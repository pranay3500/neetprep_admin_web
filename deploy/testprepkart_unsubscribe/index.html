How to request account deletion

  • Enter the email address you used to register in the app.
  • Submit this form. Our team will verify your identity and process deletion within 30 days.
  • You may also sign in to the app and use Profile → Security, or contact support if you need help.

Data deleted or retained

When your account is deleted, we remove or anonymize your profile (name, email, phone, grade, country, visa type), app preferences, favourites, message threads, demo and subscription requests tied to your account, and eligibility attempts linked to your user ID.

We may retain minimal records where required by law (for example payment or tax records) or anonymized analytics for up to 90 days, after which they are deleted or aggregated without personal identifiers.
